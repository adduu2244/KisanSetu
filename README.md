🌾 KisanSetu
Empowering Farmers Through Digital Agriculture
KisanSetu is a modern agriculture marketplace platform designed to bridge the gap between farmers and buyers. The platform helps farmers manage products, process orders, track earnings, monitor market rates, and maintain a professional digital presence through a clean and modern dashboard.

🎯 Project Vision
KisanSetu aims to digitize agricultural operations by providing farmers with a centralized platform for inventory management, order processing, earnings tracking, and market awareness.
The goal is to make farm management simple, organized, and technology-driven while improving operational efficiency for farmers.
